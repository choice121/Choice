Holding Deposit Policy

Everything you need to know about the holding deposit — what it is, when it is refundable, and how it applies.

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

What Is a Holding Deposit?

When your application is approved, you may be asked to pay a holding deposit. The holding deposit is a payment that reserves the property for you while your lease agreement is being prepared. Without it, the landlord is not obligated to hold the unit.

Amount

The holding deposit amount is communicated to you in writing by the Choice Properties team upon approval. The amount varies by property.

How It Is Applied

The holding deposit is applied toward your security deposit when your lease is executed. This is confirmed in writing in your lease agreement before you sign.

Refund Policy

The holding deposit refund eligibility depends on the timing of your request relative to payment:

TimingRefund Eligibility
Within 24 hours of paymentFull refund available upon request
24 to 48 hours after paymentPartial refund may be available — reviewed case by case
After 48 hours, or after lease is executedGenerally non-refundable

All refund decisions are reviewed by Choice Properties staff. The above are guidelines, not guarantees. Circumstances such as landlord withdrawal, material misrepresentation in the listing, or other platform-side errors may make the deposit refundable regardless of timing.

Forfeiture

If you pay the holding deposit and then decline to sign the lease without a valid reason, the deposit may be forfeited. Choice Properties will review the circumstances before making a final determination.

Non-Refundable After Lease Execution

Once the lease has been fully executed (signed by both tenant and Choice Properties management), the holding deposit is no longer refundable. From that point, it is governed by the terms of your lease agreement.

Admin Override

Choice Properties staff retain the authority to approve refunds outside of standard timelines at their discretion in cases of documented hardship, error, or extenuating circumstances.
