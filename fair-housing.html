Our commitment to fair, equal, and non-discriminatory housing practices.

Our Commitment

Choice Properties is committed to fair housing and equal opportunity for all people. We do not and will not tolerate discrimination in any form on our platform.

Federal Law

The federal Fair Housing Act (42 U.S.C. §§ 3601–3619) prohibits discrimination in the sale, rental, or financing of housing based on:

  • Race
  • Color
  • National origin
  • Religion
  • Sex
  • Familial status (including families with children under 18 and pregnant women)
  • Disability

State and Local Law

Many states and localities provide additional protections beyond federal law, including protections based on source of income, sexual orientation, gender identity, marital status, age, and other characteristics. Landlords and agents using this platform are required to comply with all applicable state and local fair housing laws.

What Choice Properties Does Not Allow

Choice Properties does not and will not:

  • Accept listings that contain discriminatory language or preferences
  • Allow filtering or sorting of applications based on protected characteristics
  • Facilitate any practice that constitutes illegal discrimination

Landlord Obligation

Every landlord and agent who posts a listing on Choice Properties agrees, as a condition of using the platform, to:

  • Comply with the federal Fair Housing Act and all applicable state and local fair housing laws
  • Not discriminate against any applicant based on any protected class
  • Apply consistent screening criteria to all applicants for the same property

Violations of this policy will result in listing removal and account suspension. Severe or repeated violations may be reported to the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) or applicable state agencies.

Tenant Rights

If you believe you have been discriminated against by a landlord or agent on this platform, you have the right to:

  • File a complaint with HUD: www.hud.gov or 1-800-669-9777
  • Contact your state's fair housing agency
  • Seek legal counsel

Choice Properties takes all fair housing complaints seriously. To report a concern, contact us at support@choiceproperties.com.

Equal Housing Opportunity

Choice Properties is an Equal Housing Opportunity platform. We are committed to ensuring that all users are treated fairly and that our platform is used in compliance with all applicable fair housing laws.
