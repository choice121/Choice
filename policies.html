1. Introduction

Welcome to Choice Properties. This document establishes the full legal, operational, and communication framework governing the use of our platform.

By accessing, using, or submitting an application through Choice Properties, you agree to all terms outlined in this document.

2. Platform Overview

Choice Properties is a rental marketplace platform that connects applicants with property owners, management companies, and authorized agents.

Our role is to:

  • Advertise rental properties
  • Collect and organize applications
  • Facilitate communication and processing
  • Provide structured workflows for rental transactions

Choice Properties does not:

  • Own listed properties
  • Guarantee approval
  • Make final leasing decisions

All final decisions are made by the property owner or their authorized representatives.

3. Application-First Model

Choice Properties operates a structured application-first system. By using this platform, you acknowledge:

  • Applications are required before property access or viewing
  • Screening is conducted prior to approval
  • Viewing may occur after approval or lease-stage processes

This system ensures: serious applicants, efficient processing, and fair and consistent evaluation.

4. Application Process

When submitting an application:

  • Your information is securely collected
  • Your application is reviewed for completeness
  • Payment coordination may occur before review
  • Applications enter formal review only after required steps are completed

Submission does not guarantee approval.

5. Application Review Timeline

Applications are processed dynamically based on completeness and responsiveness.

Typical review timeframe: 24 to 72 hours after payment confirmation.

Faster approvals may occur when applicants:

  • Submit complete and accurate information
  • Provide required documentation early
  • Respond promptly to communication
  • Demonstrate stable income and qualifications

Delays may occur if:

  • Information is incomplete
  • Additional verification is required
  • Applicant is unresponsive

6. Applicant Responsibilities

Applicants agree to:

  • Provide accurate, complete, and truthful information
  • Submit all required documentation
  • Respond to communication in a timely manner

Providing false or misleading information may result in denial or removal.

7. Application Fee & Credit Policy

Application fees are required for processing applications, are disclosed before collection, and are collected after submission through coordinated payment. Application fees are non-refundable once payment is confirmed and are required before review begins.

Reapplication Protection:

  • If denied, applicants may reapply within 30 days without paying a new fee
  • Application credits may be issued and remain valid for up to 60 days
  • Credits are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash

Full details are published in the Application Credit Policy.

8. Holding Deposit Policy

Approved applicants may be required to submit a holding deposit to reserve a property.

Holding deposit terms:

  • Must be paid within the specified deadline (typically 24–48 hours)
  • Secures the property and removes it from the market
  • Is fully credited toward move-in costs

Failure to proceed:

  • If the applicant withdraws or fails to sign the lease, the deposit may be forfeited
  • If the property becomes unavailable due to no fault of the applicant, the deposit may be refunded

Full details are published in the Holding Deposit Policy.

9. Refund & Applicant Protection Policy

Choice Properties prioritizes fairness and transparency. Applicants may qualify for refunds where:

  • The property materially differs from its advertised condition
  • The transaction cannot be completed due to verified issues
  • The property becomes unavailable after payment

Refund conditions: requests must be submitted within a reasonable timeframe and are subject to verification.

Non-refundable cases: change of mind, failure to complete required steps, voluntary withdrawal.

10. Property Information Disclaimer

Property information is provided by property owners and third parties. Choice Properties does not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or availability. Applicants are responsible for verifying details during the process.

11. Payment Terms

All payments must be completed before the next stage of processing, are considered final once confirmed, and must not be reversed without valid cause.

Unauthorized disputes or chargebacks may result in application cancellation, account restriction, or legal action.

12. Privacy Policy

Choice Properties is committed to protecting your information. We collect only necessary personal and financial data, store data securely, and share data only with relevant property parties. We do not sell personal data or share data for unrelated purposes.

Full details — including SSN handling, retention periods, breach notification, and CCPA / state privacy rights — are published in the Privacy Policy.
