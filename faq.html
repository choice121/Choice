Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting through Choice Properties.

How do I apply for a property?
Click "Apply Now" on any listing. The secure online application takes about 15 minutes. You'll need your ID, proof of income, and to be prepared to pay the application fee shown on the property listing. Once you submit, our leasing team contacts you within 24 hours to coordinate the fee payment and next steps — the application form itself does not charge your card directly.
How long does approval take?
Most applications are reviewed within 2–3 business days. You'll receive email updates at every stage and can track your status anytime using your Application ID.
What do I need to qualify?
Generally: income of 3× monthly rent, no prior evictions, and a credit check. Specific requirements vary by property and landlord. Contact us if you have questions about your situation.
Is the application fee refundable?
Application fees cover the cost of processing and evaluating your application. The fee amount is shown on each property listing before you apply. Fees are non-refundable once your application is complete and payment has been processed. However, if your application is not approved, you may be eligible for application credits you can use toward a future application — see our Application Credit Policy for details.
How do I track my application?
Use the Application Tracker with your Application ID (emailed when you applied). You can also call or text us at support@choiceproperties.com.
Can I apply for multiple properties?
Yes. You may apply to multiple listings simultaneously. Each application requires a separate fee (as listed on each property) since each involves a separate background and credit check.
Are pets allowed?
Pet policies vary by property. Look for the "Pets OK" badge on listing cards, or filter by "Pet Friendly" on the listings page. Contact us with specific questions about a property.
Still have questions?
Text us at our phone number or email support@choiceproperties.com. We respond same day.
